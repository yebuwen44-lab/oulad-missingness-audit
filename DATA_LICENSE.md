Please cite the original dataset:

Kuzilek, J., Hlosta, M., & Zdrahal, Z. (2017). Open University Learning Analytics dataset. *Scientific Data, 4*, 170171. https://doi.org/10.1038/sdata.2017.171

The raw OULAD CSV tables are not redistributed in this repository. The included processed table is derived from the anonymised public dataset and retains the original anonymised learner identifier as a dataset key. It does not contain names, contact details, or direct personal identifiers.
